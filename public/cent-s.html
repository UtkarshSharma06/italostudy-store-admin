Common Questions ?

Q: What is the competitive score for CEnT-S?

While it varies by university, a raw score of 42+ out of 55 is generally considered highly competitive for top-tier programs like Bologna or Sapienza.

Q: Can I take the CEnT-S multiple times?

Yes, usually you can take the test once per month during the open sessions. Universities typically consider your best score.
