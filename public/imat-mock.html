FAQs

Everything you need to know about the 2026 admissions cycle and ItaloStudy.

What is the IMAT Mock Test?

The IMAT Mock Test simulates the International Medical Admissions Test used by Italian universities for English-taught medical degrees.

How long is the IMAT exam?

The official IMAT exam is 100 minutes long and consists of 60 multiple-choice questions.

What subjects are covered in IMAT?

The IMAT covers General Knowledge, Logical Reasoning, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics.

How is the IMAT scored?

Candidates receive 1.5 points for correct answers, 0 points for blank answers, and -0.4 points for incorrect answers.

Are calculators allowed in IMAT?

No, calculators, periodic tables, and scratch paper are strictly prohibited during the official IMAT.

How often are new mocks released?

We release new, original mock tests in our 'Upcoming' series periodically leading up to the September exam date.

Are these past papers or original questions?

We provide both. The archive contains past papers, while the live series features completely original, never-before-seen questions.

Do these mocks reflect the latest syllabus changes?

Yes, our academic team updates the test bank annually to reflect any MUR (Ministry of University and Research) decree changes.

How can I see my ranking among other students?

By taking our live mocks, your score is placed on an anonymous global leaderboard so you can gauge your competitiveness.

Is this platform entirely free?

Yes, Italostudy's IMAT mock tests and resources are currently 100% free for all students.
